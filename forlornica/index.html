Forlornica

The official guide on mystical creatures and their origin

Forlornica is a non-profit organization that strives to seek nothing but the truth of the long lost, but still lingering creatures of the past.

Mythical entities have existed since the dawn of time, or so it is believed, quite alike mankind's own imprint and conciousness of this realm.

Along with scholars and enthusiasts all over the world we gather and share information about these elusive creatures that might be amongst us still to this day.

Some are said to possess otherworldy abilities that we cannot comprehend ourselves. People have witnessed nothing short of miraculous events that could be anything from wide-spread calamities to strange happenings such as individuals being influenced and controlled by their whim.

So join our endeavor of investigating these creatures that are bound to set you off on a bizarre adventure on uncovering the hidden truths of this world.
(It's free of charge, so don't sweat it!)
